Moon Bin - Korean: 문빈; born on January 26, 1998. Died on April 19, 2023.
Known professionally as Moonbin, was a South Korean singer, actor and dancer under the label Fantagio.
He was a member of the South Korean boy group Astro and its sub-unit Moonbin & Sanha.
